Brand Philosophy

Marvelo, aims to design sustainable and energy-efficient lighting systems. Along with the desire to enhance public spaces, workplaces, and homes.

To do so, we created lighting systems that enable users to enjoy a lighting experience that makes surrounding bright, joyful, safer and comfortable.

Incepted in 2013, we always strive to be the leaders in lighting innovation across Asia. We do so by banking on avant-garde technology to deliver finely crafted lighting fixtures.
